Job Systems in JRPGs are Awesome

div>

The other day I started and am still playing Bravely Default 2 and it has been an enjoyable experience thus far. As I was playing through, I thought back to a comment I heard in a video essay about final fantasy 5 where the person mentioned that the best RPGs have 2 levelling systems and realized just how right he was. Before I continue through let’s talk about what a job system is in a traditional JRPG/RPG. Generally, when I mention a job system, I am referring to a system where you can designate your character for a specialty or job that they can level up disconnected from their overall level. This is a bare bones explanation and has many minor different forms and exceptions but that is generally the Idea, now let’s talk about the reasons why they are awesome.
